About IDEA Week

What is human-centered design? In a time where the term is thrown out as a buzz word more often than not, the Innovation and Design Association (IDEA) at the Kellogg School of Management looks to demistify. For both design enthusiasts and the novice, IDEA has put together a series of workshops, speakers, and interactive events to help find an answer.
